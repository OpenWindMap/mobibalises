Description :
=============
Real-time display of wind information from weather stations around the world.
Those informations can be viewed in 3 differents modes : map, list, and widgets. In the list and widget modes, you can choose the weather stations to display, or those which are nextor around you.
The application also allows to view the free flight places detailled informations in France and all over the world.
This application is particularly dedicated to paragliding and hang gliding, but is also very useful for all outdoor activities wich depend on the wind (kite surfing, wind surfing, etc).
